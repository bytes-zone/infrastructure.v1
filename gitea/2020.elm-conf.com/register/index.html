Attend elm-conf!

Want to attend elm-conf? Great! Early bird tickets are on sale now and the call for speakers is open now through June 1st. Once speakers and workshops are announced, the price will go up, so get in early!

Other Ways to Attend

I'm a student!

If you're a student, please email hey@elm-conf.com with a picture of your student ID and we'll give you a free ticket.

I want to attend Elm Bridge!

If you're a member of an underrepresented or marginalized group in tech and would like to attend Elm Bridge, please email hey@elm-conf.com introducing yourself and saying why you'd like to attend the workshop. We'll be giving Elm Bridge participants tickets to the rest of the conference as well, no charge.

I want to volunteer at Elm Bridge!

If you'd like to volunteer at Elm Bridge, please email hey@elm-conf.com. Volunteers will receive a free ticket to the conference as thanks for their time.

I submitted a talk... should I buy a ticket?

If you're accepted to give a talk or lead a workshop, we'll also give you a ticket to the rest of the conference. If you're concerned about missing out on early bird pricing by waiting to see if your talk is accepted, go ahead and make the purchase; we'll refund purchased tickets for accepted speakers. If that's not possible for you, please let us know by emailing hey@elm-conf.com. We'll work something out to make sure you can attend!
